Oats

Bowl of Dry Oats!

It will dry out your mouth immediately. Only the STRONG can survive the dry, raw oat challenge.

The weak and/or sane can add hot water and tasty bits to it, maybe wait a while to let it soak in.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Hot Water
  • Peeled Bananas
  • Nut Innards

Steps

  1. Grasp a fistful of oats.
  2. Toss 'em in a bowl.
  3. Add hot water.
  4. Loiter a bit.
  5. Skin a banana and break it into coins.
  6. Harvest nut meats from the shells.
  7. Combine all and stir.
  8. Savor it.
