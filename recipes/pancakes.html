Pancakes

Flat muffin!

It belongs in your mouth immediately. Only the DIABETIC should avoid this!

Ingredients

  • Wheat dust
  • Sugar
  • Etc

Steps

  1. Swizzle the dust in water until goo.
  2. Heat up a saucepan.
  3. Drizzle it down.
  4. Wait for the edges to bubble.
  5. Flip and let it stick to the pan and burn.
  6. Feed it to the garbage.
