lasagna

Pasta wad!

It will disappoint your mouth immediately. Only the ITALIAN can survive the mound.

Have you ever wished you could burn pasta? Now you can! Take everything good about pasta and compress it into a brick.

Ingredients

  • Sheet Noodles
  • Runny Cheese
  • Italian Gravy
  • Olives

Steps

  1. Stack it up in a pyrex boat
  2. Toss some cheese at it
  3. Put it in the oven until the edges are hard enough to crack your teeth.
  4. Take it out and wait for the cheese to coagulate.
  5. Loiter a bit.
  6. Savor it.
